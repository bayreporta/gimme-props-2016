What is this place?

You have entered the magical world of Gimme Props, where we try to make sense of the 17 state propositions on the ballot this year in California. Unless you have the stamina to read through the official voter guide (yuck!) or want to read one of the many excellent "this makes hella sense" guides out there, this option is the laziest one next to nothing: answer a bunch of stupid questions and find out where you may stand on the props.

You wantz cookie?

As you play, your progress can be saved in the form of a cookie that stores on your device, so you can always come back and pick up where you stopped. You can also return here on Election Day and refresh your memory on which prop suggestions you got.
