What is this place?

You have entered the magical world of Gimme Props, where we try to make sense of the 17 state propositions on the ballot this year in California. Unless you have the stamina to read through the official voter guide (yuck!) or want to read one of the many excellent "this makes hella sense" guides out there, this option is the laziest one next to nothing: answer a bunch of strightforward questions and find out where you might stand on the props.

Answer each question by swiping left/pressing right arrow key if you agree with statement on the right or swiping right/pressing left arrow key if you agree wit the statement on the left. Based on how you answer, we'll tell you what position you might have on the prop. BTW, this is totally arbitary and likely prone to subtle bias.
