What is this place?

You have entered the magical world of Gimme Props, where we try to make sense of the 17 state propositions on the ballot this year in California. Unless you have the stamina to read through the official voter guide (yuck!) or want to read one of the many excellent "this makes hella sense" guides out there, this option is the laziest one next to nothing: answer a bunch of strightforward questions and find out where you might stand on the props.

Prop List

Swipe up/press down arrow key to navigate the list of props. Where props are similar, we combined them into one quiz (you're welcome!). When you find a prop you want to figure out a possible position on, swipe left/press right arrow key to enter the quiz. The quizzes shouldn't take any longer than a few minutes to finish.

Protip: if you see arrows pointing in a direction, you can swipe or press a directional key to move in that direction.
